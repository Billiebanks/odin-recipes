Delicious Homemade Beef Lasagne!

An extra cheesy traditional lasagne. This quick and easy recipe is perfect for a weekday meal. It freezes well for make ahead meal planning perfect a quick defrost on a busy nights too. This recipe doesnt skimp on flavour with plenty of garlic, herbs and seasoning.

Ingredients

  • Dry Lasagne Sheets

    • For The Bolognese

  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 500g beef mince
  • Tomato Paste
  • Passata
  • Oregano (Dried or fresh)
  • Basil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter

    • For the Beschamel

  • 3 Tbsp Flour (Regular or gluten free)
  • 3 Tbsp Butter
  • 3 cups Milk
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan
  • 1/2 cup Gated Mozzarella
  • 1/2 tsp Nutmeg
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Steps

Prepare the Bolognese

  1. Finely chop your onions
  2. Finely chop garlic
  3. In a large fry pan heat the olive oil
  4. Add Onions with a pinch of salt and cook for 5 minutes til browned and beginning to lightly caramelize
  5. Add Garlic and cook til fragrant. Stirring constantly to avoid burning.
  6. Add Beef Mince. Cook til browned thoroughly
  7. Add tomato paste, Passata and pinch of salt.
  8. bring to a simmer, then lower heat and simmer for atleast 20 minutes.

Prepare the Beschamel

  1. In a saucepan melt your butter, Add flour and whisk.
  2. Season with pepper and pinch of salt.
  3. While whisking, pour in milk, 1/4 cup at a time. Whisk continually, until contents become thick, and glossy.
  4. Repeat Step 3 until all the milk has been added, or until you reach your desired consistency and amount of sauce.
  5. Turn off heat and stir in parmesan, mozzarella and nutmeg.

Layer your lasagne

  1. In a rectangle baking dish spread a small amount of the bolognese and bechamel sauce along the bottom.
  2. place one layer of dried lasagne sheets on top. You can add a little extra cheese here if you like but its not necessary.
  3. Add a layer of bolognese sauce and a layer of bechamel.
  4. Continue layering to the top of the baking pan.
  5. cover with foil and cook in fan based oven at 180c for approximately 30 minutes.
  6. remove foil, add mozzarealla and continue to cook for a futher 10 mins.
  7. Let it rest for a few minutes to cool down. Serve & Enjoy!
