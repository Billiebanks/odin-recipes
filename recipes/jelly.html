Easy Peasy Jelly

Store bought Jelly in a Jiffy. Make it in the morning, ready for an afternoon snack!

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Aeroplane Jelly
  • 2 cups boiling water

Steps

  1. Pour sachet contents from Jelly box into a heat safe glass bowl or container.
  2. Add boiling water and whisk continually until granules are dissolved.
  3. pour into desired container or jars.
  4. Refrigerate for 3-4 hours, or until set.
  5. Enjoy!
